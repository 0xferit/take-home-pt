What counts as a business expense?

Examples to include

  • Coworking/office, utilities, internet, mobile
  • Equipment (laptop, peripherals), small tools
  • Software subscriptions, cloud services, domains
  • Subcontractors, professional services, training
  • Travel for work, conferences, meals with clients
  • Professional insurance (other than auto 1% freelancer liability)

Do not include

  • VAT you will reclaim (enter net of VAT)
  • Income taxes (IRS) and Social Security payments
  • Personal spending not tied to the activity

Transparent LLC: real expenses reduce taxable profit. Freelancer: expenses reduce cash but do not reduce taxable base under the simplified regime.
